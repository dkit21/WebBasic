MINI BATTLESHIP GAME

    HOW TO PLAY:

  1. Each player sets 3 location of their ships in the board, and click "'Set Location".
    (Player who sets their ships first will take the first turn)
  2. After all the ship has been set, click "Start Game" to play the game
  3. Each player guess the opponent's ship location and if attack succeed, the attacker will get +1 score
  4. Go on until a player destroy all the opponent ships

    5. *be careful, you may destroy your own ship and cause -1 score
